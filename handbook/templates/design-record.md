# Design Record Template

## Title

Short decision title.

## Status

Proposed, accepted, superseded, or rejected.

## Date

YYYY-MM-DD.

## Context

Describe the problem and constraints.

## Decision

State the decision clearly.

## Alternatives Considered

Describe reasonable alternatives and why they were not selected.

## Consequences

Document benefits, trade-offs, and follow-up work.

## Affected Files

List likely code, documentation, and validation files affected by the decision.
