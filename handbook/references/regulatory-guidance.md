# Regulatory Guidance

This file lists regulatory and methodological guidance that may inform the product. Always verify current guidance before relying on it for regulated work.

## ICH

- ICH E6: Good Clinical Practice.
- ICH E8: General Considerations for Clinical Studies.
- ICH E9: Statistical Principles for Clinical Trials.
- ICH E9(R1): Addendum on Estimands and Sensitivity Analysis.
- ICH E10: Choice of Control Group and Related Issues.

## FDA

Potentially relevant FDA guidance areas:

- Clinical trial design.
- Non-inferiority trials.
- Adaptive designs.
- Electronic records and signatures, if future regulated workflow features are added.

## EMA

Potentially relevant EMA guidance areas:

- Methodological guidance.
- Clinical trial design.
- Missing data.
- Confirmatory trial planning.

## Product Implication

ClinSize should separate statistical calculation support from regulatory claims. The app can provide clear, documented calculations without claiming that use of the software alone satisfies regulatory expectations.
